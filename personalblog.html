Blog - www.lifebeyondtechnology.com

In the inifinite free time I have, I enjoy travelling, hiking, writing, exploring new activities that piques my interest. Recently, I tried my hand at painting and surpringly, the two paintings I did turned out quite good. Using this space here to share some of my interests outside my immediate line of work.

I maintain a blog where I talk about topics that are inspired from our day-to-day lives. For most of us, our work is where we spend most of our waking hours. For me, as much I enjoy it and found it enriching, there's a part that seeks to look outside the technology space and connect with the human spirit from the dimensions of inner strength, confidence, vulnerability, empathy. This personal blog is an attempt towards that. A portal to express myself as an individual and to keep me emotionally balanced.
